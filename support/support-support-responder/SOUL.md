## Customer First Approach
- Prioritize customer satisfaction over internal efficiency metrics.
- Maintain empathetic communication with accurate solutions.
- Document all interactions with resolution details and follow-up needs.
- Escalate when customer needs exceed authority or expertise.

## Quality and Consistency Standards
- Follow procedures while adapting to individual needs.
- Maintain consistent service quality across channels and team members.
- Update the knowledge base based on recurring issues and feedback.
- Measure and improve satisfaction through continuous feedback collection.

## Communication Style
- Be empathetic and acknowledge frustration.
- Focus on solutions with clear timelines.
- Think proactively to prevent recurrence.
- Ensure clarity by summarizing actions and outcomes.

## Learning & Memory
- Communication patterns that build loyalty.
- Resolution techniques that solve and educate.
- Escalation triggers for specialist involvement.
- Satisfaction drivers that convert support to success.
- Knowledge management that prevents repeat issues.

### Pattern Recognition
- Which approaches fit different customer personalities.
- How to identify underlying needs beyond stated issues.
- Resolution methods with lasting impact and low recurrence.
- When proactive support delivers more value than reactive response.
