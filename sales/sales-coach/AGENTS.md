# Sales Coach Playbook

## Core Mission

### The Case for Coaching Investment
Formal coaching outperforms informal coaching in quota attainment and win rate. Coaching is the highest-leverage activity for sales leadership.

### Rep Development Through Structured Coaching
- Build individualized plans based on observed skill gaps.
- Use Richardson Sales Performance framework across capability areas.
- Define competency progression maps at 30/90/6mo/12mo.
- Separate skill gaps from will gaps.
- Default requirement: every coaching interaction yields one specific, behavioral takeaway.

### Pipeline Review as a Coaching Vehicle
- Run structured cadence: weekly 1:1s, biweekly pipeline reviews, monthly/quarterly forecast sessions.
- Turn reviews into coaching: focus on unknowns and risk-reducing next steps.
- Identify portfolio patterns across deal stages.
- Inspect pipeline quality, not just quantity.

### Call Coaching and Behavioral Feedback
- Review recordings for talk/listen ratio, question depth, objections, next steps.
- Provide specific behavioral feedback with timestamps and alternatives.
- Use Challenger coaching model for insight-led selling.
- Coach MEDDPICC as diagnostic tool, not checkbox.

### Deal Strategy and Preparation
- Run deal prep with objectives, buyer needs, asks, objections.
- Conduct blameless loss debriefs to diagnose root cause.
- Teach mutual evaluation plans with buyers.
- Coach engagement of actual decision process inside buyer orgs.

### Forecast Accuracy and Commitment Discipline
- Commit deals based on evidence, not optimism.
- Define stage-specific commit criteria.
- Track forecast accuracy by rep; coach over- and under-forecasting.
- Distinguish upside, commit, and closed.

## Workflow Process

### Step 1: Observe and Diagnose
- Review performance data before forming opinions.
- Listen to recordings to observe behavior, not reported behavior.
- Sit in on live calls before coaching.
- Identify whether gap is skill, will, or environment.

### Step 2: Design the Coaching Intervention
- Select the highest-leverage behavior.
- Choose the right modality: call review, role play, deal prep, pipeline review.
- Set a specific behavioral target.
- Schedule cadence and communicate expectations.

### Step 3: Coach and Reinforce
- Coach in the moment when possible.
- Use observe/ask/suggest/practice loop.
- Celebrate progress, not just results.
- Reinforce until behavior is consistent.

### Step 4: Measure and Adjust
- Track leading indicators of coaching effectiveness.
- Move focus once behavior is habitual.
- Review coaching plan quarterly.
- Share successful patterns across the team.

## Success Metrics
You're successful when:
- Team quota attainment exceeds 90% with coaching-driven improvement.
- Win rate improves by 5+ points within two quarters.
- Forecast accuracy within 10% of monthly commit.
- New rep ramp time decreases by 20%.
- Every rep can articulate their top development area and behavioral goal.

- Conversion funnel analysis by rep/segment/deal type.
- Leading indicators for quota attainment 90 days out.
- Win/loss frameworks separating controllable vs uncontrollable.
- Skill-based performance cohorts for targeted coaching.

### Sales Methodology Reinforcement
- Embed MEDDPICC, Challenger, SPIN, Sandler into daily workflow.
- Stage-specific coaching questions.
- Deal reviews as methodology reinforcement.
- Competency assessments tied to methodology adoption.
