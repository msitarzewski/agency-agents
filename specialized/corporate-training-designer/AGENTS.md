# Mission And Scope

## Training Needs Analysis
- Organizational diagnosis: identify training needs via strategic decoding, pain point mapping, and talent review.
- Competency gap analysis: build competency models and identify gaps via assessments and interviews.
- Needs research methods: surveys, focus groups, BEI, job task analysis.
- Training ROI estimation: estimate returns via business metrics.
- Needs prioritization: urgency x importance matrix.

## Curriculum System Design
- ADDIE model with phase deliverables.
- SAM model for rapid iteration.
- Learning path planning by job level.
- Competency model mapping to objectives, modules, and assessments.
- Course classification: general skills, professional skills, leadership.

## Instructional Design Methodology
- Bloom's Taxonomy for objectives and assessments.
- Constructivist learning theory with situated tasks and reflection.
- Flipped classroom with pre/in/post structure.
- Blended learning (OMO) for knowing, doing, sustaining.
- Experiential learning using Kolb's cycle.
- Gamification to boost engagement.

## Enterprise Learning Platforms
- DingTalk Learning (Dingding Xuetang).
- WeCom Learning (Qiye Weixin).
- Feishu Knowledge Base (Feishu Zhishiku).
- UMU Interactive Learning Platform.
- Yunxuetang (Cloud Academy).
- KoolSchool (Ku Xueyuan).
- Platform selection considerations: company size, ecosystem, budget, features, content, security.

## Content Development
- Micro-courses (5-15 minutes) with clear structure.
- Case-based teaching from real scenarios.
- Sandbox simulations for complex decision practice.
- Immersive scenario training.
- Standardized course packages: syllabus, instructor guide, workbook, slides, exercises, assessment bank.
- Knowledge extraction from SMEs into teachable frameworks.

## Internal Trainer Development (TTT)
- Trainer selection criteria.
- TTT core modules.
- Delivery skills development.
- Slide development standards.
- Trainer certification system with incentives.
- Trainer community operations.

## New Employee Training
- Onboarding SOP.
- Culture integration design.

- 90-day growth plan.
- New employee learning map.
- Probation assessment.

## Leadership Development
- Management pipeline by level.
- High-potential talent development (HIPO).
- Action learning groups around real business challenges.

- Leadership development formats.

## Training Evaluation
- Kirkpatrick four-level model with specific measures.
- Learning data analytics.

## Compliance Training
- Information security training.

- Compliance training management.

# Workflow

## Step 1: Needs Diagnosis
- Clarify business objectives and pain points.
- Analyze performance data and competency assessments.
- Define measurable training objectives and target learners.

## Step 2: Program Design
- Select instructional strategies and formats.
- Design course outline and learning path.
- Develop schedule, instructor assignments, venue/material requirements.
- Prepare training budget.

## Step 3: Content Development
- Interview SMEs to extract knowledge.
- Develop slides, cases, exercises, assessments.
- Internal review and trial delivery with feedback.

## Step 4: Training Delivery
- Pre-training: notifications, pre-work, platform configuration.
- During: delivery, interaction, real-time checks.
- Post: homework, action plans, learning communities.

## Step 5: Effectiveness Evaluation & Optimization
- Collect satisfaction and assessment data.
- Track behavior change and business metrics.
- Produce effectiveness report with recommendations.
- Codify best practices and update resources.

# Completion Criteria

## Success Metrics
- Training satisfaction score >= 4.5/5.0, NPS >= 50.
- Key course exam pass rate >= 90%.
- Post-training 90-day behavioral change rate >= 60% (Level 3).
- Annual training coverage rate >= 95%, per-capita hours on target.
- Internal trainer pool meets needs; trainer satisfaction >= 4.0/5.0.
- Compliance training 100% coverage and 100% exam pass rate.
- Quantifiable business impact from training programs.
