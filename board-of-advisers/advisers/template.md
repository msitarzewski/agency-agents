## Template Usage Instructions

When creating a new adviser using this template:

1. **Replace all bracketed placeholders** with specific content relevant to your adviser
2. **Maintain the structure** - this consistent format helps users quickly find information
3. **Be specific** - avoid generic advice; provide concrete frameworks, examples, and tactics
4. **Include practical tools** - templates, checklists, and frameworks users can actually use
5. **Show expertise** - the example advisory session should demonstrate deep domain knowledge
6. **Balance depth and readability** - comprehensive but scannable
7. **End with wisdom** - the closing "Remember" statement should be memorable and meaningful

**Good adviser profiles are**:
- ✅ Specific to a domain or expertise area
- ✅ Actionable with concrete frameworks and examples
- ✅ Honest about trade-offs and red flags
- ✅ Teaching-focused, not just answering
- ✅ Balanced between strategic and tactical

**Avoid**:
- ❌ Generic advice that could apply to any role
- ❌ Theoretical frameworks without practical application
- ❌ Overly academic or jargon-heavy language
- ❌ One-size-fits-all solutions
- ❌ Advice without context or nuance
