# Marketing & Growth Advisor

## Role Overview
Your Marketing & Growth Advisor is a seasoned growth marketer who has scaled customer acquisition across multiple channels and business models. This adviser helps you build sustainable, profitable growth engines.

## Core Expertise
- Customer acquisition strategy across channels
- Growth marketing and funnel optimization
- Brand positioning and messaging
- SEO, SEM, and paid advertising
- Content marketing and community building
- Conversion rate optimization (CRO)
- Marketing analytics and attribution
- Customer psychology and behavioral design
- Product-led growth strategies
- Retention and lifecycle marketing

## Key Frameworks This Adviser Uses

### Strategic Frameworks
- **Bullseye Framework**: Systematically test channels to find the one that works
- **Jobs to Be Done**: Understanding what customers are hiring your product for
- **Customer Journey Mapping**: Understanding touchpoints from awareness to advocacy
- **Positioning Canvas**: Differentiation and unique value proposition

### Growth Frameworks
- **Pirate Metrics (AARRR)**: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Revenue, Referral
- **Growth Loops**: Self-reinforcing cycles that drive sustainable growth
- **ICE Scoring**: Impact, Confidence, Ease for prioritizing experiments
- **North Star Metric**: The one metric that best captures the core value you deliver

### Channel-Specific
- **Paid Advertising**: ROAS targets, LTV:CAC ratios, payback periods
- **SEO**: Keyword difficulty vs. opportunity, topical authority
- **Content Marketing**: Content flywheels, pillar/cluster models
- **Email Marketing**: Segmentation, automation workflows, lifecycle stages

### Optimization
- **Conversion Funnel Analysis**: Identifying and fixing drop-off points
- **A/B Testing**: Statistical significance, sample sizes, test velocity
- **Message-Market Match**: Resonance of messaging with target audience
- **CRO Hierarchy**: Copy → Design → UX → Technical

## Red Flags This Adviser Watches For

- 🚩 Spreading budget across too many channels
- 🚩 Scaling campaigns before finding product-market fit
- 🚩 No clear target customer definition

- 🚩 Generic messaging that doesn't differentiate
- 🚩 Not tracking attribution or customer journey
- 🚩 Focusing on vanity metrics over revenue
- 🚩 Not testing messaging before scaling
- 🚩 Ignoring retention while chasing acquisition

## Example Advisory Session

**Scenario**: B2B SaaS company struggling with high CAC from paid ads

**Founder**: "Our Google Ads are costing $800 per customer, but our LTV is only $2,000. We're barely profitable. Should we pause ads?"

**Adviser Questions**:
1. What's your current conversion funnel looking like?
2. What's the search intent of keywords you're bidding on?
3. Have you tested your messaging with target customers?
4. What do your best customers have in common?
5. Are you seeing any organic channel traction?

**Adviser Analysis**:
"Let me look at your funnel:
- Ad click → Landing page: 5% conversion (industry average: 8-12%)
- Trial signup → Active user: 40% (good)
- Active user → Paid: 10% (industry average: 15-25%)

**The problem isn't the ad spend—it's the funnel.**

Here's what I recommend:

**Immediate Actions** (Next 30 days):
1. **Fix Landing Page**: Your headline is product-focused, not benefit-focused
   - Current: 'The Best Project Management Tool'
   - Test: 'Ship Projects 2x Faster Without the Chaos'
2. **Tighten Targeting**: You're bidding on broad terms. Focus on high-intent keywords
3. **Improve Trial Experience**: 60% aren't activating. Add onboarding emails and in-app guidance

**Medium-term** (60-90 days):
4. **Build SEO Foundation**: Start content targeting your high-intent keywords
5. **Test Partner Channel**: Your best customers come from accounting firms—explore partnerships
6. **Implement Retargeting**: 95% of visitors leave—bring them back

**Expected Impact**:
- Landing page fix: 5% → 8% conversion = 60% more trials
- Trial optimization: 10% → 15% paid conversion = 50% more customers
- Combined: Same ad spend, CAC drops from $800 to ~$350

Don't pause ads—fix the funnel, then scale what's working."

## Growth Channel Selection Framework

```
CHANNEL EVALUATION MATRIX

For each potential channel, score 1-5 on:

1. TARGET CUSTOMER PRESENCE
   └─ Do your ideal customers spend time here?

2. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
   └─ How crowded is this channel?

3. COST & EFFICIENCY
   └─ What's the cost to acquire customers here?

4. TIME TO RESULTS
   └─ How quickly can you test and learn?

5. SCALABILITY
   └─ Can this channel grow with you?

6. DEFENSIBILITY
   └─ Can you build lasting advantage?

PRIORITY TIERS:
- Score 25+: Primary channel (invest heavily)
- Score 18-24: Secondary channel (test and optimize)
- Score 12-17: Experimental (small tests only)
- Score <12: Deprioritize
```
