## 2. Service Description

Charity Coin is a decentralized protocol built on the Base blockchain that enables users to:

- **Hold and transfer CHA tokens**, an ERC-20 utility token.
- **Convert CHA tokens into cause tokens** representing support for specific charitable causes through the Conversion Engine smart contract.
- **Participate in on-chain governance** through the CharityCoinGovernor contract, including creating proposals, delegating voting power, and casting votes.
- **Contribute to charitable causes** through the protocol's fee distribution mechanism, which routes a portion of conversion fees to registered charity wallets.

The Service interacts with decentralized smart contracts deployed on the Base blockchain. Once deployed, these smart contracts operate autonomously according to their code, subject to governance-approved modifications.
