## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Blockchain Data (Publicly Available)

When you interact with the Charity Coin smart contracts, the following data is recorded on the public blockchain:

- **Wallet addresses** used to send and receive transactions.
- **Transaction details**, including CHA conversions, cause token minting, fee distributions, and governance actions (proposals, votes, delegations).
- **Token balances and transfer history.**
- **Governance activity**, including proposals submitted, votes cast, and delegation records.

This data is publicly accessible to anyone and is not collected or controlled by us. We do not have the ability to modify or delete on-chain data.

### 2.2 Data Collected Through Web Interfaces

When you access our web interfaces (websites, dApps, or dashboards), we may automatically collect:

- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device identifiers.
- **Connection information:** IP address, internet service provider, and approximate geographic location.
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent on pages, click patterns, and referral sources.
- **Wallet connection data:** The public wallet address you connect to the interface (we never have access to your private keys).

### 2.3 Data You Provide Voluntarily

If you contact us or participate in community channels, we may collect:

- **Contact information:** Email address, username, or social media handle.
- **Communication content:** Messages, feedback, support requests, and governance discussion posts.
- **Identity information:** Only if voluntarily provided or required for compliance purposes.

### 2.4 Data We Do Not Collect

- **Private keys or seed phrases.** We never request, collect, or store your private keys.
- **Financial account information.** We do not collect bank account numbers, credit card details, or other traditional financial identifiers.
- **Government-issued identification.** Unless explicitly required by applicable law for compliance purposes.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 6.1 Types of Cookies

Our web interfaces may use the following types of cookies and similar technologies:

| Type | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Essential | Required for basic functionality (e.g., wallet connection state, session management) | Session |
| Functional | Remember user preferences (e.g., theme, language) | Persistent |
| Analytics | Collect anonymized usage statistics | Persistent |

### 6.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

- View and delete existing cookies.
- Block all cookies or only third-party cookies.
- Set preferences for specific websites.

Disabling essential cookies may impair the functionality of our web interfaces. You can always interact directly with the on-chain smart contracts without using our web interfaces.

### 6.3 Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) signals sent by your browser. When a DNT signal is detected, we will not load non-essential analytics or tracking scripts.
