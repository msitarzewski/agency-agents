## 4. Semantic SEO Strategy

### Entity Associations to Build

Build topical authority by consistently associating Charity Coin with these entities:

- **Concepts**: deflationary tokenomics, on-chain transparency, charitable giving, impact investing, DAO governance
- **Technologies**: Base L2, ERC-20, Uniswap V3, smart contracts, token burning
- **Verticals**: healthcare charity, education funding, environmental conservation, clean water access, food security
- **Ecosystem**: Coinbase, Ethereum, OpenZeppelin, Base ecosystem

### Topic Clusters for Pillar-Cluster Model

**Pillar 1: "The Complete Guide to Crypto Charity"**
- Cluster: How crypto donations work
- Cluster: Tax implications of crypto giving
- Cluster: Best platforms for crypto charity
- Cluster: Blockchain transparency in nonprofits
- Cluster: Charity Coin vs traditional giving

**Pillar 2: "Understanding Deflationary Tokens"**
- Cluster: What is token burning
- Cluster: Deflationary vs inflationary tokenomics
- Cluster: How burn mechanisms create value
- Cluster: Charity Coin's burn model explained

**Pillar 3: "Impact Investing on the Blockchain"**
- Cluster: ESG criteria for crypto
- Cluster: Measuring on-chain social impact
- Cluster: DAO governance for charitable allocation
- Cluster: Cause tokens as proof of impact

### Primary Competitors

| Competitor | Strengths | Keywords They Own | Our Advantage |
|-----------|-----------|-------------------|---------------|
| The Giving Block | "donate crypto to charity", "crypto donation platform" | Branded terms, institutional partnerships | Deflationary mechanics, on-chain transparency, DAO governance |
| Gitcoin | "crypto grants", "web3 public goods" | Developer community, quadratic funding | Cause-specific tokens, burn mechanism, simpler UX |
| Binance Charity | "binance charity", "crypto charity foundation" | Brand authority, exchange traffic | Decentralized, community governed, Base ecosystem |
| UNICEF CryptoFund | "unicef crypto", "crypto for children" | Institutional trust, media coverage | Permissionless, deflationary, multi-cause |

### Keywords to Compete For

1. "crypto charity" -- Build pillar content + backlinks to rank top 5
2. "donate crypto" -- Target with conversion-focused landing page
3. "blockchain donations" -- Informational content play
4. "impact token" -- Emerging term with low competition; own it early
5. "charity token" -- Direct brand relevance; dominate this SERP

### Keywords to Avoid (Too Competitive / Low Relevance)

- "best cryptocurrency to buy" -- Too broad, wrong intent
- "crypto exchange" -- Irrelevant vertical
- "NFT charity" -- Adjacent but different product category (for now)
- "bitcoin price" -- No relevance
