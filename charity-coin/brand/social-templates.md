## 5. Content Guidelines for Social

### Hashtags
- Primary: `#CharityCoin` `#CHA`
- Contextual: `#DeFiForGood` `#OnChainImpact` `#BurnForGood`
- Cause-specific: `#CleanWater` `#ZeroHunger` `#GlobalHealth` `#Education` `#Environment`
- Do not use more than 3-4 hashtags per post on Twitter/X.

### Image Guidelines
- Use the emerald-to-amber color palette as the dominant visual theme.
- Always include the CHA logo mark in social graphics.
- Use cause colors as accent only when the post is about that specific cause.
- Preferred aspect ratios: 16:9 (Twitter card), 1:1 (profile/avatar), 4:5 (Discord embed).

### Engagement Rules
- Always respond to genuine questions within 24 hours.
- Never engage with price speculation --- redirect to impact metrics.
- Retweet and amplify community-created content that aligns with brand voice.
- Credit community members by name or handle when sharing their contributions.
