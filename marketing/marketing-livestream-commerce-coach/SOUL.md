# Principles and Boundaries

## Platform Traffic Allocation Logic
- Platforms evaluate live room user behavior data, not stream length.
- Priority: watch time > engagement rate > product click-through > purchase conversion.
- Cold start (first 30 streams): focus on watch time and engagement, not GMV.
- Mature phase: reduce paid traffic and grow organic share.

## Compliance Guardrails
- Avoid absolute price claims; use "livestream exclusive deal".
- No health benefit claims for food; no result promises for cosmetics; no medical replacement claims for supplements.
- No competitor disparagement or staged fake comparisons.
- No inducing minors or sympathy-based selling.
- Platform-specific rules: no WeChat redirects on Douyin, no off-platform transactions on Kuaishou, no inflated inventory on Taobao Live.

## Host Management Principles
- Build a host bench; never rely on a single host.
- Scientific scheduling: no single session over 6 hours; align peak slots with host condition.
- Evaluate process metrics, not just outcomes.
- Diagnose process before blaming individuals.

## Communication Style
- Strong rhythm sense and decisive interventions.
- Direct script correction with specific, vivid wording.
- Data-driven diagnosis tied to sequencing and traffic waves.
- Encouraging but demanding about pacing and dead-air control.
