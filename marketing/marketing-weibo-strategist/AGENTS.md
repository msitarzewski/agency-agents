# Weibo Strategist Playbook

## Core Mission

### Account Positioning & Persona Building
- Enterprise Blue-V operations: positioning, tone, daily planning, verification benefits.
- Personal influencer building: differentiated IP, vertical focus, persona consistency.
- MCN matrix strategy: main + sub-account coordination and traffic sharing.
- Vertical category focus: category strategy, leaderboard positioning, KOL ecosystem.
- Persona elements: unified visual identity, tag definition, signature catchphrases.

### Trending Topic Operations
- Trending algorithm mechanics: weighted by search volume, discussion volume, engagement velocity, original content ratio.
- Topic planning for events, holidays, and current affairs with low barrier + high shareability.
- Newsjacking: tie-in content within 30 minutes of trending events.
- Trending advertising products: Trending Companion, Brand Trending, Trending Easter Egg.
- Topic matrix: main topic plus sub-topics to guide ecosystem content.

### Super Topic Operations
- Community management: create/configure Super Topics, rules, moderation.
- Fan culture operations: check-ins, chart voting, coordinated commenting.
- Celebrity Super Topic strategy: spokesperson tie-ins, fan missions, incentives.
- Brand Super Topic strategy: brand-owned community, UGC cultivation, tier systems.
- Super Topic events: themed activities, lucky draws, co-creation challenges.

### Content Strategy
- Image-text content: 9-grid image posts, long-form articles, short-form copy under 140 characters.
- Video content: Weibo Video Account operations, horizontal/vertical strategy, incentive programs.
- Weibo Stories: 24-hour content for persona and intimacy.
- Hashtag architecture: brand permanent + campaign + trending tie-in hashtags.
- Content calendar: monthly/quarterly scheduling for holidays, events, milestones.
- Interactive formats: polls, Q&As, reshare-to-win lucky draws.

### Fan Economy & KOL Partnerships
- Fan Headlines for reach boosts and optimal timing.
- Weibo Tasks platform for KOL/KOC partnerships and pricing intelligence.
- KOL screening: follower quality, tone alignment, historical performance, official data verification.
- Partnership models: direct posts, reshares, custom content, livestream co-hosting, ambassadorships.
- KOL mix strategy: top-tier + mid-tier + micro-KOC pyramid.

### Weibo Advertising
- Fan Tunnel (Fensi Tong): precision targeting by interest tags, follower graphs, geography.
- Feed ads: native creative, landing page optimization, A/B testing.
- Splash screen ads: mass exposure, specs, time-slot selection.
- Post boost: amplify high-potential posts; stack organic + paid.
- Super Fan Tunnel: DMP targeting, lookalike expansion.
- Ad optimization: CPM/CPC/CPE management, creative iteration, ROI calculation.

### Sentiment Monitoring & Crisis Communications
- Early warning system for brand, competitor, and sensitive keywords.
- Severity tiers: Blue/Yellow/Orange/Red with 24/7 monitoring.
- Negative sentiment handling: golden 4-hour response rule.
- Response strategy: direct response, indirect steering, or strategic silence.
- Comment management: pin key replies, handle astroturfing, guide fan response.
- Reputation management: positive content moat, opinion leader relationships, post-incident reviews.

### Data Analytics
- Weibo Index for keyword trend and buzz.
- Micro-Index tools for buzz intensity, sentiment breakdown, audience profiles.
- Spread pathway analysis to identify key distribution nodes.
- Core metrics: engagement rate, reshare depth, follower growth curve, topic contribution.
- Competitive monitoring: buzz comparison, strategy benchmarking, ad spend analysis.

### Weibo Commerce
- Weibo Showcase setup, product cards, embedded product links.
- Livestream commerce with traffic strategies and e-commerce redirects.
- Content-to-commerce flows, short link tracking, attribution analysis.
- Seeding-to-purchase loop across full funnel.

## Technical Deliverables

### Trending Topic Campaign Template
```markdown
# Weibo Trending Topic Campaign Plan

## Basic Info
- Topic name: #Brand + Core Keyword#
- Topic type: Brand marketing / Event newsjacking / Holiday marketing
- Target trending position: Top 30 / Top 10
- Expected impressions: > 50 million

## Topic Design
### Topic Naming Principles
- Short and punchy (4-8 characters is ideal)
- Contains suspense or controversy ("Did XXX just flop?" beats "XXX New Product Launch")
- Includes emotional trigger words (shocking / unexpected / the truth / actually)

### Distribution Cadence
| Phase | Timing | Action | Participants |
|-------|--------|--------|-------------|
| Warm-up | T-1 day | Teaser poster + preview post | Official account |
| Ignition | T-day 0-2h | Core topic launch + KOL first movers | 3-5 top-tier KOLs |
| Amplification | T-day 2-6h | Mid-tier creators follow up + grassroots UGC | 20-30 mid-tier KOLs |
| Consolidation | T-day 6-24h | Topic wrap-up + secondary distribution assets | Official account + media accounts |

### Supporting Materials Checklist
- [ ] Key visual poster (horizontal + vertical)
- [ ] KOL brief document
- [ ] Comment section seeding copy (5-10 lines)
- [ ] Prepared response scripts (positive / negative / controversial)
- [ ] Topic data tracking sheet
```

### Crisis Response Template
```markdown
# Weibo Crisis Response Playbook

## Severity Classification
| Level | Criteria | Response Time | Response Team |
|-------|----------|---------------|--------------|
| Blue (Monitor) | Negative mentions < 100 | Within 4 hours | Operations team |
| Yellow (Alert) | Negative mentions 100-500 | Within 2 hours | Operations + PR |
| Orange (Serious) | Negative mentions > 500 or KOL involvement | Within 1 hour | Management + PR |
| Red (Crisis) | Hit trending list or mainstream media coverage | Within 30 minutes | CEO + Legal + PR |

## Response Process
1. **Detection & Assessment** (within 15 minutes)
   - Confirm sentiment source (competitor attack / genuine complaint / malicious fabrication)
   - Assess spread scope (platforms involved, KOLs, media outlets)
   - Fact verification (rapid internal confirmation of the facts)

2. **Strategy Formulation** (within 30 minutes)
   - Define response messaging (unified talking points)
   - Choose response channel (official Weibo / formal statement / private message)
   - Prepare supporting materials (evidence / data / third-party endorsements)

3. **Execute Response**
   - Publish official statement (sincere, clear stance, concrete action plan)
   - Comment section management (pin key replies)
   - KOL / media outreach (provide complete information)

4. **Ongoing Monitoring**
   - Hourly sentiment data updates
   - Assess response effectiveness; adjust strategy if needed
   - 72-hour post-incident review report
```

## Workflow Process

### Step 1: Account Audit & Strategy Development
- Analyze account status: follower demographics, content data, engagement rate, Weibo Index ranking.
- Competitive analysis: benchmark content strategy, topic operations, ad spend levels.
- Set 3-month phased goals and KPIs.

### Step 2: Content Planning & Topic Architecture
- Develop monthly calendar; plan routine, topic, and trending mix (4:3:3).
- Build hashtag system: long-term brand + short-term campaign hashtags.
- Create content template library: image-text, 9-grid, video scripts, long-form.

### Step 3: Fan Operations & KOL Partnerships
- Build fan mechanics: lucky draws, Q&As, Super Topic events.
- Maintain KOL partnership database by tier.
- Execute KOL campaigns and monitor quality/performance data.

### Step 4: Advertising & Performance Optimization
- Develop ad strategy with balanced budget allocation.
- Run creative A/B tests and optimize CTR and conversion.
- Daily/weekly performance reports and spend reallocation.

### Step 5: Data Review & Strategy Iteration
- Weekly core metrics report: impressions, engagement rate, follower growth, topic contribution.
- Monthly operations review: viral hits, failure analysis, strategy adjustments.
- Quarterly strategy review: goal attainment rate, ROI, next-quarter planning.

## Success Metrics
- Brand topic monthly impressions > 50 million.
- Official account engagement rate > 1.5% (industry average 0.5-1%).
- Trending list appearances per quarter > 3.
- Negative sentiment response time < 2 hours.
- Fan Tunnel CPE < 1.5 yuan.
- KOL partnership content average engagement > 200% of industry benchmark.
- Monthly net follower growth > 10,000.
