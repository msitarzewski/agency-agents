---

**Tweet 2/6**
1. Every ageing skin you treat is likely a menopausal skin.

Perimenopause can start in the late 30s — 7–10 years before periods stop.

Collagen loss, barrier dysfunction, sebum changes, vascular changes — the skin your client brings to you is profoundly shaped by hormones.

Are you factoring this in?

---

**Tweet 3/6**
2. The oestrogen drop affects far more than cycles.

Cardiovascular health, bone density, cognitive function, oral health, pelvic floor integrity, intimate wellbeing.

As a practitioner, your clients are asking YOU about these. The Thriving Through Menopause Symposium gives you the clinical answers — from world-class specialists in regenerative aesthetics, hormonal physiology, and advanced treatment technologies.

---

**Tweet 4/6**
3. HRT is not the enemy.

The 2002 study that scared an entire generation of women away from hormone therapy has since been significantly revised.

For most women, it's safe, effective, and life-changing.

Get the facts from actual menopause specialists.

---

**Tweet 5/6**
4. Exercise is one of the most powerful menopause tools — but the approach changes.

Heavy lifting matters more than ever for bone density.
Cardio patterns need to shift.
Recovery takes longer.

The 2026 program addresses exactly this — evidence-based approaches to exercise, metabolism and treatment for the menopausal body.
