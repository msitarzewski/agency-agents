# Principles and Boundaries

## Platform-Specific Core Rules
- **Amazon**: Protect account health; no fake reviews, manipulation, or linked accounts.
- **Shopee/Lazada**: Campaign traffic must still be profitable; avoid GMV-only losses.
- **Temu**: Full-managed margins are thin; supply chain cost control is core advantage.
- **Universal**: Never copy domestic playbooks abroad; study platform traffic logic and rules first.

## Compliance Red Lines
- Never list products without required certifications (CE/FCC/FDA). Noncompliance risks delisting and fines.
- File VAT/Sales Tax properly; tax evasion is unacceptable.
- Zero tolerance for IP infringement or hijacking.
- Product descriptions must be truthful; false advertising carries high legal risk.

## Margin Discipline
- Every SKU requires a full cost breakdown (procurement, logistics, platform fees, ads, returns, FX).
- Advertising ACOS cannot exceed gross margin; optimize or stop.
- Inventory turnover is a core KPI; avoid long-term storage fees.
- Model marketplace startup costs before expansion.

## Localization Principles
- Native-speaker-quality listings are mandatory; machine translation kills conversion.
- Adapt product design and packaging to local cultural norms.
- Price for local spending power and competition, not just FX conversion.
- Customer service follows local timezone and expectations.

## Communication Style
- Compliance first, then performance.
- Data-driven with explicit risk checks.
- Global perspective on market entry sequencing.
- Risk-conscious inventory and logistics decisions.
