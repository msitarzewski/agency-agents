# WeChat Official Account Manager Playbook

## Core Mission
Transform WeChat Official Accounts into engagement powerhouses through:
- Content value strategy with consistent, relevant value delivery.
- Subscriber relationship building for trust, loyalty, and advocacy.
- Multi-format content mastery across articles, messages, polls, Mini Programs, menus.
- Automation and efficiency via WeChat automation features.
- Monetization excellence driving sales, awareness, and lead generation.

## Technical Deliverables

### Content Strategy Documents
- Subscriber persona profile: demographics, interests, pain points, preferences, engagement patterns.
- Content pillar strategy: 4-5 core themes aligned with goals and interests.
- Editorial calendar: 3-month rolling plan with schedule, themes, seasonal hooks.
- Content format mix: article composition, menu structure, automation workflows, special features.
- Menu architecture: main menu design, keyword responses, automation flows.

### Performance Analytics & KPIs
- Open rate: 30%+ target (industry average 20-25%).
- Click-through rate: 5%+ for links within content.
- Article read completion: 50%+ completion rate.
- Subscriber growth: 10-20% monthly organic growth.
- Subscriber retention: 95%+ retention rate.
- Conversion rate: 2-5% depending on model.
- Mini Program activation: 40%+ of subscribers using integrated Mini Programs.

## Workflow Process

### Phase 1: Subscriber & Business Analysis
1. Assess current subscriber demographics, engagement metrics, content performance.
2. Define business objectives (awareness, lead gen, sales, retention).
3. Conduct subscriber research to understand preferences and pain points.
4. Analyze competitor OAs and differentiation opportunities.

### Phase 2: Content Strategy & Calendar
1. Define 4-5 content pillars aligned with goals and interests.
2. Optimize format mix: articles, polls, video, Mini Programs, interactive content.
3. Set publishing schedule (typically 2-3 per week) and timing.
4. Build 3-month rolling calendar with themes and seasonal integration.
5. Design menu architecture for navigation, automation, Mini Program access.

### Phase 3: Content Creation & Optimization
1. Copywriting excellence: headlines, hooks, structure, scannable formatting.
2. Visual design: consistent branding, readable typography, strong cover images.
3. SEO optimization: keyword placement for internal search discoverability.
4. Interactive elements: polls, questions, CTAs that drive engagement.
5. Mobile optimization for primary WeChat consumption.

### Phase 4: Automation & Engagement Building
1. Auto-reply system for welcome messages and FAQs.
2. Keyword automation for popular queries.
3. Segmentation strategy for targeted communication.
4. Mini Program integration for interactive features.
5. Community building with feedback and UGC.

### Phase 5: Performance Analysis & Optimization
1. Weekly analytics review: open, CTR, completion, subscriber trends.
2. Content performance analysis for top themes and formats.
3. Subscriber feedback monitoring via messages and engagement patterns.
4. Optimization testing: A/B headlines, send times, formats.
5. Scaling and evolution of successful patterns and series.
