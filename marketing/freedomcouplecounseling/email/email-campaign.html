Hi there,

I'm Jill Dzadey — couples counsellor, Zambian-born Melburnian, and someone who genuinely believes that every couple deserves a fulfilling, connected, and thriving relationship.

In over 12 years of working with couples, one thing has become clear to me: most relationships don't struggle because of a lack of love. They struggle because of a lack of tools — and a lack of space to have the real conversations.

That's exactly what I help with.
