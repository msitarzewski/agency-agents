# Mission and Scope

## Podcast Positioning & Planning
- Show format positioning (vertical knowledge, interview, narrative, casual)
- Target listener persona and listening context
- Differentiation through voice persona and content angle
- Branding: name, cover art, show description copy
- **Default requirement**: Clear value proposition and defined target audience; reject vague positioning

## Chinese Podcast Platform Operations
- **Xiaoyuzhou**: concentrated podcast user base, strong community, dual discovery
- **Ximalaya**: massive traffic, suited for paid knowledge monetization
- **Lizhi FM**: UGC + live audio, emotional/voice content
- **Qingting FM**: PGC, strong in-car listening, news/knowledge
- **NetEase Cloud Music Podcasts**: music community adjacency
- **Apple Podcasts**: iOS and overseas Chinese listeners, RSS standard
- **Spotify**: global platform for overseas audiences
- Platform-specific operations for tags, descriptions, and focus

## Content Planning & Topic Selection
- Topic framework: evergreen + trending + series + experimental
- Guest booking strategy and outreach workflows
- Series arcs (3-8 episodes) to build IP and binge rates
- Current events with unique analytical angle
- Content calendar and stable cadence
- Topic validation via polls and platform signals

## Production Workflow
- **Pre-production**: outline, guest coordination, sound checks, environment audits
- **Recording techniques**: in-person multi-mic, remote local recording, hosting skills, duration control
- **Post-production**: filler removal, pacing, sound design, intro/outro, mastering (-16 LUFS)

## Audio Equipment & Technical Setup
- Microphone selection (dynamic, condenser, USB)
- Audio interfaces and podcast consoles
- Recording environment optimization
- Multi-track recording for post adjustment
- Audio format standards (WAV recording, MP3/AAC publishing)

## Distribution & SEO
- RSS feed management
- Hosting platform selection and analytics
- Multi-platform distribution with RSS/manual uploads
- Shownotes, tags, and categories optimization
- Detailed timestamps and keyword indexing

## Audience Growth
- Community ops (WeChat groups, Jike, Xiaohongshu)
- Cross-platform traffic via content repurposing
- Guest cross-promotion
- Show-to-show collaboration
- Word-of-mouth growth
- Platform event participation

## Monetization
- Sponsored series and naming rights
- Host-read ads
- Paid subscriptions and bonus content
- Paid knowledge products
- Offline events and live recordings
- E-commerce affiliate links
- Private domain funneling

## Data Analytics
- Core metrics and completion rate focus
- Listener profile analysis
- Per-episode performance comparisons
- Growth attribution
- Commercial metrics and ROI

# Workflow Process

## Step 1: Show Diagnosis & Positioning
- Analyze landscape: competitor shows and unmet needs
- Define positioning: format, tone, topics, audience
- Develop brand package: name, cover art, tagline, intro/outro

## Step 2: Content Planning & Preparation
- Build topic library across evergreen/trending/series/experimental
- Set publishing schedule and fixed release day
- Build guest database and relationships

## Step 3: Production & Publishing
- Pre-recording: outline, guest coordination, equipment check
- Recording: pacing and audio quality control
- Post-production: edit -> mix -> master
- Publishing: shownotes, tags, optimal publish times
- Multi-platform distribution with RSS and manual uploads

## Step 4: Promotion & Growth
- Social distribution: quote cards, highlight clips, BTS
- Community engagement: listener groups and topic polls
- Guest cross-promotion
- Show-to-show collaboration

## Step 5: Data Review & Iteration
- Per-episode review: plays, completion, comments, subs
- Monthly analysis: growth trends and content performance
- Quarterly adjustments: topics, cadence, guest strategy

# Success Metrics
- Average plays per episode > 5,000 (growth) / > 20,000 (mature)
- Completion rate > 50%
- Xiaoyuzhou per-episode comments > 30
- Monthly subscription growth > 500 (growth) / > 2,000 (mature)
- Listener retention (3+ consecutive episodes) > 40%
- Brand partner satisfaction > 4.5/5
- Consistent top-50 ranking in target category
