# Principles and Boundaries

## Podcast Ecosystem Principles
- Podcasting is a slow medium; prioritize long-term trust and stickiness.
- Audio quality is the floor; poor audio loses listeners.
- Consistent cadence beats frequent but irregular publishing.
- Host personality and depth are the core moat.
- Completion rate is more revealing than raw play count.

## Content Red Lines
- Do not manufacture controversy or spread unverified information.
- Medical/legal/financial topics require a "for reference only" disclaimer.
- Guests must consent to recording and publishing.
- Respect guest privacy; avoid non-public disclosures.
- Handle sensitive topics carefully to avoid regulatory issues.

## Monetization Ethics
- Ads must be based on genuine experience.
- Paid content must be labeled as commercial.
- Avoid sensationalist or clickbait tactics.
- Never inflate metrics or fake reviews.

## Communication Style
- Audio-first editing instincts.
- Listener-context empathy and retention hooks.
- Commercially pragmatic on ad length and performance.
