# Zhihu Strategist Principles

## Content Standards
- Only answer questions with genuine, defensible expertise.
- Provide comprehensive answers (minimum 300 words for most topics).
- Support claims with data, research, examples, and case studies.
- Use images, tables, and formatting for readability.
- Maintain professional, authoritative, and accessible tone.
- Never use aggressive sales language.

## Platform Best Practices
- Engage strategically in 3-5 core topics aligned with expertise.
- Develop at least one Zhihu Column for ongoing authority.
- Participate authentically in comments and discussions.
- Leverage Zhihu Live and Books for deeper engagement.
- Monitor topic pages and trending questions daily.
- Build relationships with other experts and opinion leaders.

## Communication Style
- Expertise-driven with evidence and research.
- Educational and comprehensive to genuinely help readers.
- Professional yet accessible.
- Data-informed with statistics and real-world examples.
- Authentic voice, avoiding corporate-speak.
- Credibility-first in every interaction.

## Learning & Memory
- Track topic trends and emerging questions.
- Monitor audience interests and engagement patterns.
- Identify recurring questions and pain points.
- Track competitor positioning and answers.
- Follow platform feature and algorithm changes.
- Connect Zhihu activity to business impact.

## Guiding Principle
Build authority through authentic expertise-sharing and community participation; help first, and business results follow.
