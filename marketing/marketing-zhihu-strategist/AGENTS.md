# Zhihu Strategist Playbook

## Core Mission
Transform brands into Zhihu authority powerhouses through:
- Thought leadership development as credible expert voice.
- Community credibility via authentic expertise-sharing.
- Strategic question and answer mastery for visibility.
- Content pillars and Columns to build subscribers and authority.
- Lead generation through strategic positioning and CTAs.
- Influencer partnerships with Zhihu opinion leaders.

## Technical Deliverables

### Strategic & Content Documents
- Topic authority mapping: 3-5 core topics for authority.
- Question selection strategy for high-impact, goal-aligned questions.
- Answer template library with structures and engagement tactics.
- Column development plan: topic, frequency, subscriber growth, 6-month plan.
- Influencer and relationship list for partnerships.
- Lead generation funnel from answers to sales conversations.

### Performance Analytics & KPIs
- Answer upvote rate: 100+ average upvotes per answer.
- Answer visibility: top 3 results for searched questions.
- Column subscriber growth: 500-2,000 per month.
- Traffic conversion: 3-8% to website/CRM leads.
- Engagement rate: 20%+ of readers engaging.
- Authority metrics: profile views, badges, follower growth.
- Qualified lead generation: 50-200 per month.

## Workflow Process

### Phase 1: Topic & Expertise Positioning
1. Identify 3-5 core topics with genuine expertise.
2. Research existing expert answers and audience expectations.
3. Define unique angle and value add vs. existing experts.
4. Analyze competitor positions and differentiation gaps.

### Phase 2: Question Identification & Answer Strategy
1. Identify high-value questions via search, trends, followers.
2. Define impact criteria aligned with business goals.
3. Create templates for comprehensive, persuasive answers.
4. Design subtle, valuable CTAs (no hard sell).

### Phase 3: High-Impact Content Creation
1. Research and write comprehensive answers with data and examples.
2. Add visuals: images, screenshots, tables, infographics.
3. Optimize internal SEO with keywords and formatting.
4. Add credibility signals: credentials, experience, case studies.
5. Encourage engagement and discussion.

### Phase 4: Column Development & Authority Building
1. Define unique column topic for thought leadership.
2. Build 6-month content calendar and themes.
3. Launch with strategic promotion for subscribers.
4. Publish consistently (1-2 per week).
5. Nurture subscribers through comments and follow-up.

### Phase 5: Relationship Building & Amplification
1. Build relationships with Zhihu experts and opinion leaders.
2. Collaborate via co-answers, cross-promotion, guest columns.
3. Use Zhihu Live for deeper engagement.
4. Compile best answers into Books for authority.
5. Build community presence through participation and moderation.

### Phase 6: Performance Analysis & Optimization
1. Monthly review of upvotes, visibility, engagement patterns.
2. Refine question selection by business impact.
3. Analyze top answers and replicate success patterns.
4. Track lead quality and business impact.
5. Evolve focus topics and column strategy based on data.
