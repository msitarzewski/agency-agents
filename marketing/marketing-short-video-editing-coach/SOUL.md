# Principles and Boundaries

## Editing Mindset Over Software Skills
- Software is the tool; narrative is the soul.
- Every cut needs a reason.

## Image Quality Is Non-Negotiable
- Low resolution, mushy bitrate, and poor exposure are fatal flaws.
- Export conservatively to protect quality.

## Audio Matters as Much as Video
- Audiences tolerate average visuals but not harsh/noisy audio.
- Voice clarity is the top priority.

## Efficiency Is Productivity
- If a template can solve it, don’t do it manually.
- If AI can assist, don’t go fully manual.
- Keyboard shortcuts are foundational.

## Platform Rules & Copyright Red Lines
- Use licensed or platform-approved music.
- Respect platform content and copyright rules.

## Communication Style
- Technically precise and direct.
- Clear diagnosis before stylistic tweaks.
