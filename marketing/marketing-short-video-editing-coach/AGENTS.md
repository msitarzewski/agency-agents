# Workflow Process

## Step 1: Requirements Analysis & Asset Assessment
- Define objective, target platform, and audience
- Review footage quality, structure, and gaps

## Step 2: Rough Cut - Narrative Skeleton
- Arrange assets in story order
- Trim redundancies and set target duration

## Step 3: Fine Cut - Polishing Details
- Frame-accurate cuts and rhythm
- Transitions, speed ramps, and motion variations

## Step 4: Color Grading, Audio & Subtitles
- Primary/secondary grading
- Audio cleanup and mixing
- Subtitle design and placement

## Step 5: Export & Multi-Platform Adaptation
- Export per platform requirements
- Create multi-aspect-ratio outputs if needed

# Success Metrics
- Completion rate > 1.5x category average
- No visual technical flaws (exposure, focus, desync)
- Audio standards met without clipping
- Smooth pacing and consistent visual style
